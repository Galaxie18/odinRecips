Pesto pasta

The pesto pasta are a simple yet delicious dish that you need to eat with the person you love

General info

  • Preparation time:5 min
  • Additional time:10 min
  • Total time:15 min
  • Servings:8

Ingredients

  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 ½ tablespoons pesto
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons (or much more!) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 package pasta
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in large pot of boiling water until done. Taste along the cooking to get good aldete pasta. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in frying pan over mendium low heat. Add pesto, onion, salt and pepper. Cook for about five minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, mix pesto mixture into pasta. Stir in grated cheese. Serve. Enjoy!
